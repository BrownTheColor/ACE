The Problem.

As population increases so does the number of the homeless population. Coorelated with the rise in homelessness, drugs and alchohol are rampant and the crime rate reflects this. Our effort is too reduce the number of people living on the street, thus decreasing the stated statistics.

Here are our steps to success below!

  1. First Contact - Locating the individual is the most important part of the process. Once the client is located one of our volunteers or partners will make first contact and discuss with the client if they are or are not interested in our program. If the client is interested we proceed with step 2. Try it now!
  2. Transportation - Our volunteers will transport the client from the location of pickup to our best suited house for that individual depending on behavior and their personal needs. Try it now!
  3. Placement - Once dropped off too the facility the client will start the admission process which takes less then 1 hour. After, the client will be shown to their bedroom and start their recovery journey! Try it now!
